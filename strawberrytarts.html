Strawberry Tarts

Choose a different recipe

Ingredients


Puff Pastry Sheets
Strawberrys
Brown Sugar
Butter

Production


1. Preheat oven
2. Cut Puff Pastry into quarters
3. Cut Strawberries into quarters
4. Put strawberries into pastry
5. Put a generous amount of brown sugar in
6. Fold each corner of each pastry to the top
7. Melt the butter and brush it on the pastry with a pastry brush
8. Put the pastry onto a tray and put in the oven (use non stick spray or baking paper)
9. Cook until golden
10. Serve warm
